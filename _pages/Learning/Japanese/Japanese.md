## ぽい vs らしい

ぽい used when \[subject\] being like the \[object\] but not really is the object
月歌は子供ぽい　〇
めぐみんは関西人ぽい　 ×

らしい　 used when \[subject\] being like the \[object\] and it really defined as the object
月歌は子供らしい　 ×
めぐみんは関西人らしい　〇
